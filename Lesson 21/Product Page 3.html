Portae Cradle

Product Description

As with beer, pouring wine from just the right angle can make a world of difference. That’s where wine cradles come in: They allow you to pour your wine from a 20-degree angle, ensuring that sediment doesn’t get into your wineglass or decanter. Sommelier and winemaker André Hueston Mack loves the Portae Wine Cradle, featuring a leather sling and an aluminum frame, not only for being functional, but also “1,000 percent a work of art!”
