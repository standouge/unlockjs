Home-X Keepsake Wine Cork Holder

Product Description

A fun and innovative way to keep and cherish all of your wonderful wine memories, and a great way gift to give a bottle of wine to your friends and family. They'll never forget this clever present. Just drop your treasured corks inside. This wine corks holder feature a 3 bottle holder and a small chalkboard so you can make it a personalized gift by writing a a special message on the chalkboard (Chalk not included). Corks are easy to remove if needed via a cage opening at the top. Holds over 100 corks and measure approx 13 inch tall 7 inch wide and 6 inch deep.
